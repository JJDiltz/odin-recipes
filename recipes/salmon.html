Salmon recipe

Delicious Salmon with lemon butter sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 6oz salmon filets
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • lemon
  • heavy cream
  • garlic
  • butter
  • dry white wine
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. heat oil in pan on med-high
  2. while thats heating, dice up garlic and juice the lemon into a bowl.
  3. in a sauce pot, melt butter and add garlic
  4. when garlic is fragrant, add heavy cream and bring to simmer
  5. salt and pepper the salmon filets and place in oil, flip after 2min
  6. after cream has reduced add white wine and reduce again to burn off alcohol
  7. add juiced lemon to white cream sauce and simmer for 3 min
  8. remove salmon after flipped and cooked again
  9. put white sauce over salmon filet and enjoy!
