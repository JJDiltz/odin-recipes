lasagna recipe

Pictured above is some delicious lasagna

Ingredients

  • 16oz Ricotta cheese
  • 12oz Parmasean cheese
  • 48 oz canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 pack Lasagna noodles
  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Olice oil
  • 5 clove Garlic
  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb sausage
  • 1 med onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • coriander

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and sausage in pot
  2. While meat is browning, dice up onion and garlic
  3. After meat is brown, add in Onion and Garlic, salt and pepper
  4. After that becomes fragrant add canned diced tomatoes and tomato sauce
  5. simmer that for 20 mins
  6. add oregano, basil and continue to simmer for two hours -- stir every 25min
  7. mix the different cheeses together with 2tbps salt and pepper
  8. begin layering in a baking tray, cheese first, then noodles then sauce
  9. repeat until all sauce and cheese is used with noodles
  10. bake at 425F for 30min
