Burger recipe

smash burger in all its glory

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • brioche bun
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cheddar cheese
  • high burn temp oil

Steps

  1. take 8oz of ground beef roll into a ball
  2. turn your grill onto its highest temp and get it smoking hot
  3. place cast iron on grill, and add oil
  4. when its smoking hot, place ball onto pan and press it firmly flat
  5. add your salt and pepper
  6. after 2 min, flip and add cheese
  7. remove when done and prepare your burger how you like.
